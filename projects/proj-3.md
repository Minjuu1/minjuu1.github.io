# Overview

This coursework is an project-based course that focuses on learning the ADDIE process, which is the foundation of instructional design, and applying it to design programs.

Our team designed an training program about online class for high school teachers.
Due to COVID-19, all schools have transitioned to online learning environments, and this has led to many difficulties arising from technical issues. In particular, the role of teachers in conducting classes and managing students has become even more significant. However, due to the barriers using online environment and tools, they had trouble keeping their learning system. Therefore, we tried to address the problem of using online tools, which is an issue that can be solved through education.
