Minju Yoo

I am a senior undergraduate student at Ewha Womans University, pursuing a double major in Educational Technology and Software. I am currently working as a undergraduate research intern in KIXLAB. My research interests lie at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Educational Technology, and Learning Sciences, with a particular focus on personalized tutoring, teaching augmentation, and interactive learning systems.

My research aims to leverage computing technology to enhance learning experiences, with a specific focus on improving interactions in classroom and collaborative learning(Computer-Supported Collaborative Learning). I am passionate about identifying and solving challenges in educational interactions through innovative technological solutions.
