Minju Yoo

I am a senior undergraduate student at Ewha Womans University, pursuing a double major in Educational Technology and Software. I am currently working as a undergraduate research intern in KIXLAB.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Educational Technology, and Learning Engineering. I aim to enhance learning experiences through computing technology, particularly through interactive learning activities using conversational agents. Also, I am interested in supporting instructors to effectively integrate these technologies into their classroom and augment their teaching practices. As an early-career researcher, I hope to explore various forms of technology-enhanced learning experience, from AI-driven educational interactions to collaborative learning.
